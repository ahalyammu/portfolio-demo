Hey I am CHETAN BHAGAT

I am an Indian author, columnist, and speaker, best known for my popular contemporary fiction novels. I had credited with making English novels more accessible to a wider Indian audience, especially among young readers. Some of my well-known works include "Five Point Someone," "One Night @ the Call Center," "2 States," "Half Girlfriend," and "The 3 Mistakes of My Life."
I had garnered both praise and criticism. While some appreciate my ability to connect with a broad readership, others critique my writing style and portrayal of certain themes. Nonetheless, my impact on Indian literature and my popularity among readers is undeniable.
